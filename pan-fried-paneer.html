Pan Fried Paneer

Pan Fried Paneer

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Total Time: 1 hour
Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

Paneer

  • 12 ounces paneer, sliced 1/2 inch thick
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 11/2 to 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon rice flour
  • 1 tablespoon chickpea flour
  • avocado oil, for frying
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala (optional)
  • pickled red onion (below)
  • pineapple salsa (below)
  • handful mixed greens

Pickled Red Onions

  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Pineapple Salsa

  • 2 to 3 cups diced pineapple
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • juice of 2 limes
  • 1 tablespoon orange juice

Method

  1. Make the pineapple salsa: mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Put the white vinegar, salt and pepper into a jar with the onions and shake. Refrigerate for at least 10 minutes.
  3. Lay out paneer slices in a single layer on a baking sheet. Evenly dust the first side with half the salt, chili powder, turmeric and rub the spices into the paneer. Turn over and repeat with the remaining spices.
  4. Combine the rice flour and chickpea flour in a small bowl. Dip each paneer slice into the bowl, shake off excess, and lay onto baking sheet.
  5. Heat avocado oil in a medium frying pan over medium heat. When the oil is hot, slide in half the slices and fry for about a minute on each side, or until golden brown. Remove with a slotted spoon and transfer to a paper towel lined plate. Repeat for second batch.
  6. Remove the sliced onions from the vinegar.
  7. Assemble the dish: lay a few mixed greens on the plate. Add the paneer slices and sprinkle with chaat masala if desired. Top with a spoonful of pineapple salsa and a couple of onion slices.
  8. Enjoy!
