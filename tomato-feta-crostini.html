Tomato Feta Crostini

Tomato Feta Crostini

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Total Time: 1 hour 5 min
Yield: 6 to 8 servings

Ingredients

Crostini

  • 2 pounds cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 6 ounces feta, crumbled
  • 2 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
  • 2/3 cup olive oil, divided
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons basil, julienned
  • 20 to 25 toasted baguette slices, 1/2 inch thick

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Transfer tomatoes to baking sheet, drizzle with 1 tablespoon olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper. Roast for 35 minutes.
  3. Up to an hour before serving, combine the shallots, garlic, and balsamic vinegar in a bowl. Set aside for 10 minutes. Whisk together the remaining olive oil and some salt and pepper. Add the tomatoes and basil. Mix gently and set aside.
  4. Pulse the feta and cream cheese in a food processor. Add 1/4 cup olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Assemble the crostini: spread each slice of bread with a generous amount of the whipped feta mixture. Top with a spoon of the tomato mixture.
  6. Enjoy!
