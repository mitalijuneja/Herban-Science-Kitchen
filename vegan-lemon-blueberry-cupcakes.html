Vegan Lemon Blueberry Cupcakes

Vegan Lemon Blueberry Cupcakes

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Total Time: 1 hour 5 min
Yield: 10 to 12 cupcakes

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • 210 grams spelt flour, divided
  • 120 grams organic cane sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 90 grams blueberries, plus more for garnishing
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest, plus more for garnishing

Frosting

  • 14 ounces canned full fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 2 tablespoons melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • 2 ounces blueberry preserves, pureed

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Line 10 to 12 muffin tins with paper or silicon liners.
  2. In a small bowl add the blueberries and 2 tablespoons of the flour. Toss to coat and set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the spelt flour, cane sugar, baking soda, and sea salt. Add the coconut oil and almond milk and whisk until combined. Add the lemon juice and zest and stir a few times with a wooden spoon until just combined. Gently fold in the blueberries. Quickly pour into the prepared muffin tins.
  4. Bake for about 16 to 18 minutes, until the top of the cupcakes are slightly golden and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Remove from the oven and let sit for about 10 minutes. Remove the cupcakes from the pan and let cool completely on a cooling rack.
  6. Make the frosting: Without shaking it, remove the coconut milk can from the refrigerator. Scrape the thick cream at the top and place into a stand up mixer with a whisk attachment. Add the maple syrup, coconut oil, lemon juice, and blueberry preserves into the bowl with the coconut cream and whip for about 2 minutes, until fluffy. Refrigerate frosting until ready to serve.
  7. Assemble the cupcakes: frost the cupcakes with the refrigerated frosting. Top with a few blueberries and sprinkle with lemon zest.
  8. Enjoy!
