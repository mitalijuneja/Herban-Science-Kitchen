Mediterranean Salad

Mediterranean Salad

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 0 min
Total Time: 15 min
Yield: 4 to 6 servings

Ingredients

Salad

  • 2 cups cooked Israeli couscous
  • 2 cups thinly sliced kale
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup pitted kalamata olives, halved
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced pickled banana peppers
  • 2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup sliced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 persian cucumbers, diced
  • 5 ounces feta cheese, cut into 1/4 inch thick slices
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

Method

  1. In a bowl, combine the couscous, kale, onions, olives, banana pepper, 1 teaspoon of the red wine vinegar, and 1 tablespoon of the olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Toss to coat
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the bell peppers, tomatoes, and cucumbers. Add the remaining 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and salt to taste. Toss to coat.
  3. On a large platter, arrange the couscous mixture in the center, then top with the cucumber mixture. Arrange the feta slices on top. Sprinkle the oregano and some salt and pepper over the top.
  4. Enjoy!
