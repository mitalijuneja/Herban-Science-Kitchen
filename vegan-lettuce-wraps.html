Vegan Lettuce Wraps

Vegan Lettuce Wraps

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Total Time: 35 min
Yield: 6 to 8 lettuce wraps

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sweet chili sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, peeled
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled

Lettuce Wraps

  • sauce (above)
  • 314 ounces extra firm tofu, pressed and cut into cubes
  • 4 ounces rice noodles
  • butter lettuce leaves
  • chopped peanuts, cilantro, and lime for serving

Method

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Soak in cold water to stop the cooking process.
  2. For the sauce: add all ingredients into a food processor and pulse until smooth.
  3. Heat some olive oil in a nonstick pan over medium high heat. Add the tofu and cook on all sides until golden brown. Add about half the sauce and let it cook for another 2 to 4 minutes. Transfer tofu to a bowl.
  4. Drain noodles. Add some more olive oil into the pan and add the noodles and the remaining sauce. Stir fry for about 1 to 2 minutes, tossing to coat. Remove from heat and toss with the tofu.
  5. Assemble the lettuce wraps: Fill the butter lettuce leaves with the noodles and tofu. Top with chopped peanuts, cilantro and a squeeze of lime juice.
  6. Enjoy!
