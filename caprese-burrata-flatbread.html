Caprese Burrata Flatbread

Caprese Burrata Flatbread

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Total Time: 1 hour 5 min
Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

Flatbread

  • 1 loaf ciabatta bread, halved lengthwise
  • 1/4 cup pesto
  • 2 cups heirloom cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • flaky sea salt and pepper
  • 8 ounces burrata cheese

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Arrange the tomatoes on a baking sheet, drizzle with 1 tablespoon of the olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Roast in the oven for 35 to 45 minutes.
  3. Reduce the oven temperature to 375 degrees.
  4. Spread the pesto on the cut side of the bread. Place on a baking sheet. Transfer to the oven and bake for 10 minutes or until the bread is golden and toasted.
  5. In a medium bowl, toss the tomatoes with the remaining 2 tablespoons of olive oil, balsamic vinegar, 1 tablespoon of the basil, and garlic. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Break the burrata over the toasted bread and spread evenly. Top with the tomatoes mixture and finish with the remaining tablespoon of basil. Season to taste with flaky sea salt and pepper.
  7. Enjoy!
