Asian Tofu Salad

Asian Tofu Salad

Prep Time: 1 hour
Cook Time: 30 min
Total Time: 1 hour 30 min
Yield: 2 to 4 servings

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3/4 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2/3 cup plus 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • pinch of red pepper flakes
  • 14 ounces extra firm tofu, pressed and cut into cubes
  • 10 ounces mixed greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 11 ounces mandarin oranges

Method

  1. Put the soy sauce, rice vinegar, ginger, honey, sesame oil, garlic and 2/3 cup of the olive oil into a food processor. Pulse until emulsified.
  2. Put the tofu cubes into a bowl and with half the dressing. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Put the white vinegar, salt and pepper into a jar with the onions and shake. Refrigerate for at least 10 minutes.
  4. Remove tofu from the dressing and pat dry. Heat the remaining 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a nonstick pan over medium high heat. Cook the tofu on all sides until golden brown. Remove tofu from pan onto a paper towel lined plate.
  5. Assemble the salad: Remove the sliced onions from the vinegar. In a large bowl, add the mixed greens, tomatoes, sliced onions, tofu, and mandarin oranges. Add the remaining half of the dressing. Toss to coat.
  6. Enjoy!
