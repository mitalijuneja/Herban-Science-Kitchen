Moroccan Couscous

Moroccan Couscous

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Total Time: 55 min
Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

Couscous

  • oven roasted butternut squash (below)
  • 8 ounces israeli couscous
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3/4 cup mint
  • 3/4 cup cilantro
  • 2 cups slivered almonds, toasted
  • 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon coriander
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1/2 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 6 ounces arugula

Oven Roasted Butternut Squash

  • 12 ounces butternut squash, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Arrange the butternut squash on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt, garlic powder, cumin, coriander, and smoked paprika. Roast for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the israeli couscous according to package directions, drain, and set aside.
  4. In a large pan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the onions and garlic and cook for 5 to 8 minutes until translucent and browning.
  5. Add the olive oil, salt, pepper, coriander, cumin, and turmeric to the pan and stir. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Add the lemon juice, couscous, and almonds. Stir again.
  6. Transfer the couscous mixture to a large mixing bowl. Add the cilantro, mint, and arugula. Gently fold in the butternut squash and feta.
  7. Enjoy!
