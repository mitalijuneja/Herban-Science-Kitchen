Pesto and Cheese Stuffed Shells

Pesto and Cheese Stuffed Shells

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Total Time: 50 min
Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

Stuffed Shells

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 32 ounces marinara sauce
  • 2/3 pound jumbo pasta shells
  • 11 ounces whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup basil pesto, divided
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 ounces mozzarella, torn
  • basil, torn

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Use the olive oil to lightly oil a 9Ã—13 inch baking dish.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat and boil the pasta shells until al dente according to package directions.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta, shredded mozzarella, 1/2 cup of the pesto, salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
  4. Spoon 3/4 of the marinara sauce into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Working one pasta shell at a time, use a small spoon to fill about 1 tablespoon of the cheese mixture into each shell and place the shells into the baking dish as you go.
  5. Spoon the remaining marinara sauce and 1/4 cup pesto over the shells. Add the mozzarella.
  6. Bake in the oven for 25 to 30 minutes, until the cheese is melted and lightly browned on top. Let cool 5 minutes before serving and sprinkle basil over top.
  7. Enjoy!
