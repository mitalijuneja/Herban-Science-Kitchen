Roasted Tomato Sandwich

Roasted Tomato Sandwich

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Total Time: 1 hour 15 min
Yield: 6 small sandwiches

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 2 roma tomatoes, sliced into thirds
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 persian cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, thinly sliced
  • handful of spring greens
  • cilantro cashew sauce (below)
  • pickled red onions (below)
  • 6 mini pretzel buns

Cilantro Cashew Sauce

  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 3/4 cup roasted salted cashews
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • juice of 2 limes
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Pickled Red Onions

  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Place tomato slices on baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  3. Roast for 35 to 45 minutes. Let cool.
  4. Put the white vinegar, salt and pepper into a jar with the onions and shake. Refrigerate for at least 10 minutes.
  5. Make the cilantro cashew sauce: Puree all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.
  6. Remove the sliced onions from the vinegar.
  7. Assemble the burgers: cilantro cashew sauce, a few mixed greens, a couple of onion slices, roasted tomato slice, and a couple of avocado and cucumber slices on top.
  8. Enjoy!
