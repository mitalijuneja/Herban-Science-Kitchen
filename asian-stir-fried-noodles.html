Asian Stir Fried Noodles

Asian Stir Fried Noodles

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Total Time: 45 min
Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

Noodles

  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • 14 ounces extra firm tofu, pressed and cut into cubes
  • 8 ounces white mushrooms, sliced
  • stir fry sauce (below)
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 1/2 carrots, julienned
  • 1 1/2 cup red, orange, and yellow bell peppers, julienned
  • 3/4 cup scallions, chopped, divided
  • chopped peanuts
  • lime juice

Stir Fry Sauce

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic

Method

  1. Make the stir fry sauce: add all ingredients to a food processor and pulse until smooth.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook the rice noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside in a bowl of cold water.
  3. In a nonstick skillet over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of the olive oil and cook the tofu on all sides until golden brown. Add a little bit of the stir fry sauce to get a browned crust. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil and the mushrooms. Sauté for about 3 minutes. Add some of the stir fry sauce and continue sautéing for another 5 to 7 minutes, until browned and reduced. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. In the same pan, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil and the carrots and bell peppers. Stir fry over medium high heat heat for about 2 to 3 minutes, until tender crisp.
  6. Drain the noodles and add them into the pan with the veggies. Add about half of the remaining stir fry sauce and 1/2 cup of the scallions. Stir fry until thoroughly mixed.
  7. Assemble the dish. Add the noodles in a bowl. Top with some of the remaining scallions, some of the tofu, and some of the mushrooms. Finish with some chopped peanuts and a squeeze of lime juice.
  8. Enjoy!
