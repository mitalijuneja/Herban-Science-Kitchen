Moroccan Carrot Tacos

Moroccan Carrot Tacos

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Total Time: 50 min
Yield: 6 to 8 tacos

Ingredients

Tacos

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground fennel
  • 1 1/2 pounds carrots, cut into 2 inch strips
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon orange zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup cilanto, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup mint, finely chopped
  • 4 ounces goat cheese
  • mint and garlic yogurt (below)
  • 6 to 8 tortillas (corn or flour)

Mint and Garlic Yogurt

  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 3/4 cup whole milk greek yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup mint, finely chopped

Method

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil. Add the garlic, cumin, and fennel and cook over low heat until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the carrots, salt, and pepper, and cook over medium to medium-high heat until tender, about 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from heat, add the orange juice and zest, lemon juice and zest, cilantro, and mint, and mix until combined.
  2. Make the mint and garlic yogurt: In a small skillet over low heat, toast the cumin seeds until fragrant and slightly darker in color, about 2 minutes. Cool, and then grind in a spice grinder. In a medium sized bowl, add the yogurt, garlic, cumin, lemon juice and zest, salt, and mint. Mix until combined.
  3. Assemble the tacos: On each tortilla, spread some goat cheese and Then, add some of the carrots and top with some of the mint and garlic yogurt.
  4. Enjoy!
