Sweet Potato Soup with Sage Pesto

Sweet Potato Soup with Sage Pesto

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Total Time: 60 min
Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

Sweet Potato Soup

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cups low sodium veggie broth
  • 3-4 sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 pinch cayenne
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 14 ounces coconut milk
  • 8 ounces burrata

Sage Pesto

  • 1/3 cup and 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup sage leaves
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup roasted pistachios
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • salt
  • red pepper flakes

Method

  1. Make the soup: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Then add the shallots and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the thyme and butter and cook for another 30 seconds. Pour in the veggie broth and add the sweet potatoes, nutmeg, cayenne, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, and then reduce to medium-low heat. Cover and simmer until the sweet potatoes are tender, about 15 to 20 minutes. Blend the soup and stir in the coconut milk. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  2. Make the sage pesto: Heat 2 tablespoons of the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sage and cook until crisp and fragrant, about 2 minutes. In a food processor, pulse the remaining 1/3 cup of olive oil, spinach, pistachios, and parmesan until finely chopped. Add the sage, season to taste with salt and red pepper flakes and pulse to combine.
  3. Assemble the soup bowls: Ladle the soup into bowls. Top with a spoon of sage pesto and some pieces of burrata.
  4. Enjoy!
