Brussels Sprout Pizza

Brussels Sprout Pizza

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Total Time: 30 min
Yield: 10 inch pizza

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 10 inch par-baked pizza crust
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • zest and juice of 1/2 meyer lemon
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 75 grams Brussels sprouts, trimmed and thinly sliced
  • 6 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced and drained on paper towels
  • 3 ounces goat cheese, crumbled
  • a few thin slices of red onion
  • a pinch of red chile flakes

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees. Place the pizza crust on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium-size bowl, combine the garlic, olive oil, lemon zest and juice, and salt. Add the Brussels sprouts and toss to combine.
  3. Layer the mozzarella slices on the pizza crust. Top with the Brussel sprouts, goat cheese, red onion, and chile flakes.
  4. Bake in the oven for 8 to 10 minutes. Slice into sixths.
  5. Enjoy!
