Three Cheese Flatbread with Spicy Tomato Chutney

Three Cheese Flatbread with Spicy Tomato Chutney

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Total Time: 1 hour 5 min
Yield: 4 to 6 servings

Ingredients

Flatbread

  • 1 pound pizza dough, room temperature
  • 1 cup whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons grated lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup basil, julienned

Spicy Tomato Chutney

  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, peeled
  • 21/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 15 ounces diced tomatoes

Method

  1. For the chutney: in a food processor, pulse the garlic, ginger, salt, balsamic vinegar, sugar, olive oil, cumin, and pepper. Pour into a medium saucepan over high heat and add the tomatoes. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer and let it thicken for about 40 minutes. Stir occasionally. Using the back of a wooden spoon, gently break up the tomatoes as they soften. Season with salt to taste and let cool.
  2. For the flatbread: preheat oven to 450 degrees. In a bowl, combine the ricotta, oregano, lemon zest, and salt. Stretch out pizza dough on an oiled baking sheet into an 11 inch by 16 inch rectangle. Spread evenly with the ricotta mixture, leaving a 1/2 inch border. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the ricotta. Bake for 17 to 20 minutes, until golden brown. Remove from oven and sprinkle with parmesan and basil.
  3. Cut flatbread into pieces and serve with chutney.
  4. Enjoy!
