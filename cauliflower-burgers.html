Cauliflower Burgers

Cauliflower Burgers

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Total Time: 1 hour
Yield: 10 burgers

Ingredients

Burgers

  • 3/4 cup cooked quinoa
  • 6 cups cauliflower florets
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 3/4 cup shredded pepperjack cheese
  • 3 flax eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

Toppings

  • sliced pepperjack cheese
  • pretzel buns
  • vegan chipotle mayo
  • cilantro cashew sauce (below)
  • cilantro lime slaw (below)

Cilantro Cashew Sauce

  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 3/4 cup roasted salted cashews
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • juice of 2 limes
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Cilantro Lime Slaw

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions
  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • juice of 2 limes
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 3 1/2 cups shredded purple and green cabbage

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Transfer cauliflower to baking sheet, drizzle with 1 tablespoon olive oil, sprinkle with 1 teaspoon each cumin and chili powder.
  3. Roast for 25 minutes. Let cool.
  4. Pulse cauliflower in food processor until chopped into small pieces. Combine with quinoa, salt, garlic, cheese, almond flour, flax eggs, and 1 teaspoon each cumin and chili powder. Form 10 patties.
  5. In an oiled pan over medium heat, cook burgers until golden brown on each side, top with a slice of pepperjack cheese and let it melt.
  6. Make the cilantro cashew sauce: Puree all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.
  7. Make the cilantro lime slaw: Pulse ingredients (from oil to lime juice) in food processor until chopped. Add greek yogurt and pulse again. Toss the dressing with the cabbage.
  8. Assemble the burgers: cilantro cashew sauce, cauliflower burger, cilantro lime slaw, and vegan chipotle mayo on top.
  9. Enjoy!
